🔔 AlgoBell — Your Personal Coding Contest Reminder

Tired of missing coding contests? AlgoBell sends you smart, timely reminders before contests start on all major competitive programming platforms.

🏆 SUPPORTED PLATFORMS
• Codeforces
• LeetCode
• CodeChef
• AtCoder
• HackerEarth
• HackerRank

⏰ SMART REMINDERS
• Set custom reminder offsets — 10 min, 30 min, 1 hour, or 1 day before
• Choose your notification channel — In-App Alarm, Email, Web Push, or WhatsApp (Pro)
• Auto-subscribe to contests from your favorite platforms
• Never configure reminders manually again

📊 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
• View upcoming contests from all platforms in one place
• Monthly attendance stats and performance tracking
• Filter contests by platform, difficulty, and duration

🚀 KEY FEATURES
• Real-time contest data synced from official sources
• Loud in-app alarm with snooze option — works like a phone alarm
• One-tap subscribe/unsubscribe to any contest
• Beautiful dark-themed UI designed for developers
• Contest Explorer with search and advanced filters
• Google Sign-In for quick setup

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• No ads, no tracking
• Your data stays secure
• Open and transparent privacy policy

Whether you're preparing for ICPC, Google Code Jam, or just practicing on LeetCode — AlgoBell makes sure you never miss a contest again.

Download now and start winning! 💪
